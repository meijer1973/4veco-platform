## Data integrity notes

Protected reference data must not change in R8.1.

R8.1 may edit:

- `references/schemas/quality-issue.schema.json`
- `references/data/qc/reference-quality-issues.json`
- report/dashboard/RAG tooling
- generated report and inventory surfaces

R8.1 must not hand-edit:

- `references/machine/`
- `references/external/`

## Baseline risks

- The current `quality-issue.schema.json` is present but too loose to express the authority boundary and proof-to-close discipline needed now.
- Existing generated reports expose issues, but they do not yet provide a single dashboard/RAG-friendly internal issue layer.
- QC records could be misread as curriculum authority unless the model makes `internal_only`, `curriculum_authority: false`, `student_facing_exposure: false`, and `primary_evidence: false` explicit.

## Stop conditions

Stop if:

- the issue model needs hand edits to `references/machine/` or `references/external/`;
- categories expand into a general issue tracker;
- QC issues are treated as primary evidence or curriculum authority;
- generated report diagnostics are used as proof instead of source-backed evidence;
- any validation or sprint-bundle check fails and cannot be fixed within R8.1 scope.
