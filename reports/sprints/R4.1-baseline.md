# Sprint R4.1: Baseline

## Plan reference

`docs/sprints/R4.1-plan.md`

## Baseline summary

R4.1 starts after R3.2 completed CLI-only dependency and zero-needs corrections.

Baseline observations:

- Unit registry: 192 total units, 190 live units.
- Term registry: 225 terms.
- Current unit `terms` fields use Dutch display strings rather than canonical slug IDs.
- Initial mapping probe found 98 unique unit term strings.
- Initial mapping probe found 96 exact safe mappings and 2 unresolved terms: `alternatieve kosten` and `schaarste`.

## Data integrity notes

`references/machine/` may change only through the validated R4.1 migration script. `references/external/` is out of scope and must remain unchanged.

## Known risks

- Some current unit term strings may refer to real terms missing from `begrippen.json`.
- Synonyms and deprecated forms must not be treated as permission to change meaning.
- The unit validator must accept canonical term IDs before migrated unit terms can validate.
