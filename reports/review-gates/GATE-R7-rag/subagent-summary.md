# GATE-R7 Review-Lens Summary

## Summary

The three review lenses agree on `pass_with_conditions` as the proposed R7.4 gate status.

## Retrieval Lens

R7 retrieval passes the fixed evaluation set, but ranking is still lexical and can include broad low-score results. This is acceptable for internal review and evaluation, not for student-facing decisions.

## Authority-Ranking Lens

Authority metadata is preserved. Generated reports remain non-primary evidence. Whole-graph authority remains false.

## Evidence-Surfacing Lens

Source paths, evidence IDs, and warning flags are visible. Retrieved chunks should route reviewers to source inspection rather than replace it.

## Proposed Conditions

- Keep retrieval internal or explicitly non-authoritative.
- Preserve source authority, edge status, and warning metadata in every consumer.
- Keep student diagnostics, adaptive routing, student-facing AI, automatic sequencing, mastery decisions, and summative assessment blocked.
- Split target-exercise chunks before widening teacher-facing use.

## Gate State

This is a packet summary only. The gate is not closed.
